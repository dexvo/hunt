Common Issues

  • Can't log in: Reset your password via the app. If that doesn't work, email us.
  • Messages not sending: Check your internet connection. Try closing and reopening the app.
  • Location not working: Enable location permissions in your device settings.
  • Subscription issues: Managed through App Store/Play Store. Email us if you need help.
  • Report a user: Long-press their profile or message and select "Report."
