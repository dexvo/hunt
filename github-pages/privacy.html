Privacy Policy

Lynx Privacy Policy


Last Updated: March 16, 2026


Introduction


Pockito Labs ("we," "us," "our") operates Lynx, a location-based social networking and dating application for gay men. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.


By using Lynx, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.


Information We Collect


Information You Provide

  • **Account Information:** Email address, phone number, date of birth, username
  • **Profile Information:** Photos, bio, interests, preferences, height, body type, relationship status
  • **Location Data:** GPS location (approximate or precise, based on your settings)
  • **Messages:** Direct messages sent and received through the app
  • **Verification Data:** Government ID or selfie photos (if you choose to verify your account)

    • Information We Collect Automatically

  • **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address
  • **Usage Data:** App interactions, features used, time spent, profiles viewed, matches, messages sent
  • **Location Data:** Approximate location based on IP address (even if GPS is disabled)

    • Information from Third Parties

  • **Social Media:** If you sign up via Apple, Google, or Facebook, we receive basic profile info (name, email, profile photo)
  • **Payment Processors:** Transaction details (not full credit card numbers) when you purchase subscriptions

    • How We Use Your Information


    We use your information to:

  • **Provide the Service:** Display your profile to nearby users, facilitate matches and messages
  • **Personalize Experience:** Show relevant profiles based on your preferences and location
  • **Safety & Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, spam, harassment, and violations of our Terms
  • **Communication:** Send you notifications, updates, marketing messages (you can opt out)
  • **Analytics:** Understand usage patterns to improve the app
  • **Legal Compliance:** Respond to law enforcement requests, enforce our Terms, protect rights and safety

    • How We Share Your Information


    With Other Users

  • Your profile (photos, bio, age, location distance) is visible to users in your area
  • Your exact location is **never** shared; only approximate distance (e.g., "500m away")
  • Your online status and "last seen" may be visible (you can disable this in settings)

    • With Service Providers

    We share data with third parties who help us operate Lynx:

  • **Cloud Hosting:** Supabase (database), Amazon Web Services (file storage)
  • **Analytics:** Firebase Analytics, PostHog
  • **Payment Processing:** Stripe, Apple App Store, Google Play
  • **Email Services:** Resend (transactional emails)
  • **Customer Support:** Zendesk

    • These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for services they provide to us.


    With Law Enforcement

    We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protect against fraud or security threats
  • Prevent harm to individuals or the public
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

    • Business Transfers

    If Lynx is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to the new owner.


    Your Choices and Rights


    Account Settings

  • **Location Sharing:** Enable/disable GPS location, control who sees your distance
  • **Profile Visibility:** Hide your profile temporarily (Incognito Mode for premium users)
  • **Notifications:** Manage push notifications, email preferences
  • **Data Access:** Request a copy of your data via Settings → Privacy → Download My Data

    • Deletion

  • **Delete Account:** Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, messages, and matches
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., transaction records, fraud prevention)

    • Marketing Opt-Out

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the link in any email
  • Disable push notifications in your device settings

    • Regional Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have additional rights:

  • **Europe (GDPR):** Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, objection
  • **California (CCPA):** Know what data is collected, delete data, opt-out of sales (we don't sell data)
  • **Brazil (LGPD):** Access, correction, deletion, portability

    • To exercise these rights, email [email protected].


    Data Security


    We use industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

    • However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use Lynx at your own risk.


    Data Retention


    We retain your data:

  • **Active Accounts:** As long as your account is active
  • **Deleted Accounts:** 30 days (to allow recovery if accidental), then permanently deleted
  • **Legal/Safety Data:** Fraud/abuse records retained for up to 7 years to prevent repeat offenders
  • **Anonymized Analytics:** Indefinitely (cannot be linked back to you)

    • Children's Privacy


    Lynx is 18+ only. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we discover a minor's account, it is immediately deleted. If you believe a minor is using Lynx, report it to **[email protected]**.


    International Data Transfers


    Lynx operates globally. Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your own, including the United States. We ensure adequate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) for cross-border transfers.


    Changes to This Policy


    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

    • Continued use of Lynx after changes constitutes acceptance.
