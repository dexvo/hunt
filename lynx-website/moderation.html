Content Moderation Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

BREEDX is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all users. This Content Moderation Policy outlines the standards we enforce and the actions we take to protect our community.

Age Requirement

  • All users must be 18 years of age or older
  • We employ automated and manual verification systems to prevent underage access
  • Accounts found to belong to minors are immediately and permanently banned

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on BREEDX:

Profile Content (Photos, Bios, Username)

  • Explicit nudity (exposed genitals, full frontal nudity)
  • Sexually explicit acts or pornographic content
  • Images of minors or content sexualizing minors
  • Impersonation of others or use of others' photos without consent
  • Hate speech, discriminatory language, or symbols promoting violence
  • Promotion of illegal activities (drugs, prostitution, human trafficking)
  • Spam, commercial solicitation, or advertising
  • Graphic violence or self-harm content

Direct Messages

  • Harassment, threats, or intimidation
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery (revenge porn, hidden camera footage)
  • Content involving minors in any sexual or suggestive context
  • Bestiality or sexual content involving animals
  • Spam or fraudulent schemes
  • Solicitation for prostitution or illegal services
  • Sharing private information without consent (doxxing)

Private Sexual Content: Adult users (18+) may consensually exchange sexually explicit photos and videos (including nudity and sexual acts) in private direct messages. Such content must remain consensual and legal. Users can report unwanted sexual content at any time.

User Reporting

Users can report violations directly within the app:

  • Profile Reports: Tap any profile → Report → Select reason
  • Message Reports: Long-press any message → Report
  • Block Feature: Users can immediately block anyone from contacting them

All reports are reviewed within 24 hours. Emergency reports (threats of violence, minors, illegal activity) are escalated immediately.

Enforcement Actions

Violations result in the following actions based on severity:

First Offense (Minor Violations)

  • Warning notification
  • Temporary content removal
  • Account review

Repeat or Serious Violations

  • 7-day account suspension
  • Permanent content removal
  • Profile visibility restriction

Severe Violations

  • Immediate and permanent account ban
  • IP and device ban to prevent re-registration
  • Report to law enforcement (for illegal activity involving minors, violence, or trafficking)

Automated Detection

BREEDX employs automated systems to detect:

  • Inappropriate profile photos (AI image moderation)
  • Spam patterns in messages
  • Known scam/fraud language
  • Banned email domains and phone numbers

Automated flags are reviewed by human moderators before final action is taken.

Photo Verification

  • Users may be required to verify their identity through live photo verification
  • Verified profiles receive a badge to increase trust
  • Failure to verify when requested may result in profile restrictions

Appeals Process

Users may appeal moderation decisions:

  1. Email hello@pockitolabs.com with subject "BREEDX Appeal - [Username]"
  2. Include account details and reason for appeal
  3. Appeals are reviewed within 72 hours
  4. Final decisions are communicated via email

Content Removal Requests

Users may request removal of their own content or report content that violates their privacy by contacting hello@pockitolabs.com.

Law Enforcement Cooperation

BREEDX cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies investigating:

  • Child exploitation
  • Human trafficking
  • Violent threats
  • Illegal activity

User data may be disclosed to authorities when legally required or when necessary to prevent imminent harm.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of material changes via in-app notification and email.
